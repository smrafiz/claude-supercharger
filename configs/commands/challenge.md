Stress-test this decision: $ARGUMENTS

Be adversarial. The goal is to find flaws, not confirm the decision. Agreement is not useful here.

**Step 1 — Restate**
What exactly is being decided? State it precisely, not as interpreted.

**Step 2 — Assumptions**
List 3–5 core assumptions this decision depends on. What must be true for it to work?

**Step 3 — Failure modes**
Give 3 specific, concrete scenarios where this decision leads to a bad outcome. Be specific — name the situation, not a vague risk category.

**Step 4 — Strongest alternative**
What is the best competing option? Describe it concretely. Why might someone prefer it?

**Step 5 — Blind spots**
What has this analysis not considered? What context, constraint, or stakeholder is missing?

**Step 6 — Verdict**
One of: PROCEED / RECONSIDER / NEEDS MORE INFO
One sentence explaining why.

Output format:
```
DECISION: [restated precisely]

ASSUMPTIONS:
1. [assumption]
2. [assumption]
3. [assumption]

FAILURE MODES:
1. [scenario] — [consequence]
2. [scenario] — [consequence]
3. [scenario] — [consequence]

STRONGEST ALTERNATIVE: [description + why someone would choose it]

BLIND SPOTS: [what's missing from this analysis]

VERDICT: [PROCEED / RECONSIDER / NEEDS MORE INFO]
REASON: [one sentence]
```
